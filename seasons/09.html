After the eighth season finale "Existence", Fox Mulder goes into hiding. Dana Scully is again reassigned to the FBI Academy, and Monica Reyes becomes John Doggett's new FBI partner in the X-Files office. Doggett asks Scully for help on a case involving an EPA official, Carl Wormus, who died after his car was forced off a bridge by a woman he picked up. Doggett and Walter Skinner travel to a reclamation plant, looking for links between Wormus' work and death. After doing an illegal autopsy on Wormus, Alvin Kersh sends Brad Follmer to locate Doggett. The investigation at the reclamation plant leads to an unknown woman, whose identity is later revealed to be Shannon McMahon, one of Doggett's former Marine associates.
